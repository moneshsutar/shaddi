Wedding Party A Big Party

Everyone invited to the wedding is usually invited to the reception, you know Indian receptions have a reputation as par-tays. Receptions normally start close to seven and go until around midnight. Sometimes there's a farewell for the bride and groom, who venture off in a fancy car or something comparable.

Wedding outfit Traditional Indian Outfit

"Honestly, for Indians, weddings are like a huge fashion show. "You have a different outfit for every single event." The bride and bridesmaids wear saris or lengha; the groom and groomsmen wear a sherwani, which is a long top and pants, and the groom usually dons a turban. "The groom gets to do as many outfit changes as the bride, which is pretty cool. but members of the bridal party are allotted fewer looks. Each attendee's sari or lengha (the skirt version) feels as radiantly splendid as the last with bright colors and gorgeous embellishments.
